Samsonite

Founded in 1910, Samsonite is the world leader in superior travel bags, luggage and accessories. Renowned for breakthrough research and development, Samsonite builds trusted, innovative and sustainable products that elevate the way we travel. Inspired by the past and built for the present, Samsonite’s purpose is to pack confidence into every single journey and believes that the people who experience the world will move it forward. Strength of a Product: Samsonite’s durable, long-lasting luggage is made to the highest standards and won’t let you down. Timelessness of a Brand: Samsonite is inspired by the past, devoted to the present and built to inspire a multi-generational future​. Power of Travel: Samsonite believes in the transformative power of travel and gives you the confidence to get out there. Social Compliances: Samsonite works to ensure that its supply chain reflects its values as a company. Samsonite expects its suppliers to treat their workers fairly, to provide them with a safe and healthy work environment and to protect environmental quality. ​​
