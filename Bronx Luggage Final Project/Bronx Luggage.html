Categories

Laptop Bags

A laptop bag is a padded bag that is slightly larger than the laptop and it is designed to protect your laptop from scratches.
handbags

A purse, also known as a handbag, is a bag used to carry money, wallets, phones, and other daily essentials.
Business Bags

Today business bags are a combination of a laptop bag and a document case.
Rolling Duffle Bags

Rolling duffel bags combine the pliability of a duffel bag with the wheeled functionality of a piece of carry-on luggage or a checked suitcase.
Carry-on Luggages

Carry-on Luggage refers to the type of luggage that passenger allowed to carry along in the passenger compartment of a vehicle instead of a separate cargo compartment.
Hard Shell Luggages

Hard shell bags are typically made with “split construction”–meaning the bag zips down the middle and opens into two shallower main compartments, like a clamshell.
